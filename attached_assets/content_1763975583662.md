# Tourist Places in India (Region-wise) with Best Time to Travel

## 1. North India

### Major Tourist Destinations

#### Hill Stations & Mountain Regions

-   Manali (Himachal Pradesh)
-   Shimla (Himachal Pradesh)
-   Dharamshala & McLeod Ganj (Himachal Pradesh)
-   Dalhousie (Himachal Pradesh)
-   Nainital, Ranikhet, Almora (Uttarakhand)
-   Mussoorie (Uttarakhand)
-   Auli (Uttarakhand)
-   Leh--Ladakh (UT of Ladakh)

#### Nature & Trekking Spots

-   Kasol, Kheerganga
-   Spiti Valley
-   Valley of Flowers
-   Jim Corbett National Park

#### Cultural & Historical

-   Delhi
-   Agra (Taj Mahal)
-   Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur, Jaisalmer (Rajasthan)

### Best Time to Visit North India

-   Himalayan Hill Stations: **March--June** & **Sept--Nov**
-   Snow Destinations (Auli, Manali, Gulmarg): **Dec--Feb**
-   Rajasthan & Delhi: **Oct--March**
-   Ladakh: **June--Sept**
